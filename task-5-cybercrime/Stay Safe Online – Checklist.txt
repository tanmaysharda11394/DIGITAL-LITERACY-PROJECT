A. Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India):
 1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and enable two-factor authentication.
 2. Keep your social media accounts private and avoid sharing personal details publicly.
 3. Verify links before clicking—especially from unknown emails or messages.
 4. Only download apps from official stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store) and check reviews before installing.
 5. Log out of accounts when using public or shared computers (e.g., in college labs or cyber cafés).
 6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
 7. Regularly update your phone apps to fix security vulnerabilities.
 8. Be cautious when granting app permissions.

B. Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
 1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
 2. Always double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before making payments.
 3. keep in mind, you do not need to enter a UPI PIN to receive money, only to send.

C. If something goes wrong:
 1. Immediately report cyber fraud or harassment on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in.
 2. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930.
