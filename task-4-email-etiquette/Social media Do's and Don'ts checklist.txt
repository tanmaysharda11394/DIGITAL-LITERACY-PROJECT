Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use

1. Do think before you post—consider how it may affect your future.
2. Do protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
3. Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4. Do use respectful and polite language in your posts and comments.
5. Do maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your values and goals.




Don'ts for Responsible Social Media Use

1. Don't share sensitive personal details like passwords, address, or phone number publicly.
2. Don't engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.
3. Don't post inappropriate or offensive content.
4. Don't forward every piece of information without checking its authenticity.
5. Don't spend excessive time on social media that affects your health and studies.
